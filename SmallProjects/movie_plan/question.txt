Alex has a movie colection and he needs to watch them all ASAP.
The duration of the movies are given in an array: durations[n], n is the number of movies.
Each movie is 1.01 to 3.00 units time.
Alex can watch no more than 3.00 units time.
Alex has to watch all movies within the least number of days.
Alex  doesn't watch half movies.
Please find the minimum number of days Alex needs to watch all the movies.

Example:

n=5

durations[1.90, 1.04, 1.25, 2.5, 1.75]

minimum days Alex needs: 3 (1.04+1.90, 1.25+1.75,2.5)
